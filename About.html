About Me


My name is Josh, and I'm currently attending college pursuing a double major in Computer Science, and Psychology. Technology and how people interact and communicate through them has always interested me, and has led me to my current studies and hobbies. Back in 2013, I started creating gaming videos on YouTube under the username JuniperGaming with a few friends. Over time, the prospect of content creation has grown to be more and more of a passion of mine, up until this day.

Starting in the summer of 2013, I started to mess around with Adobe Photoshop creating graphics for my content creation, 100% self taught. Now that a few years have passed, I've learned a lot through my time on YouTube creating content and continuing to make graphics. Over time, people became interested in commissioning me for my graphics, which led me to where I am now in the realm of graphic design!

In addition to graphic design, I'm interested in all things related to technology, as I touched upon. Technology is chaning the way our society works, and this has fascinated me. As a result, this has led me to studying both topics of Computer Science, and Psychology - the way humans perceive and relate to the technology around us, and applying it to everyday life. I also have experience working in a computer support enviornment, assisting people with troubleshooting computer problems, and setting up new computer systems.
